Our Story

Welcome to Zafar Homeopathic Store & Clinic

Dr. Muneeba Malik Awan is a Government-registered Homoeopathic Physician with 8+ years of clinical experience. She completed her DHMS from Noor Memorial Homoeopathic Medical College, Lahore and has served patients across the city with dedication and care.

Before starting her own clinic, she gained valuable experience at Baba Homeopathic Clinic, Boharwala Chowk, Lahore. Her clinic is located in the heart of Lahore at Boharwala Chowk, near the Orange Line Metro Station — easily accessible for patients from across the city.

The clinic specializes in natural, side-effect-free homoeopathic treatment for kidney stones, skin diseases, hair loss, stomach issues, and blood pressure. Whether you visit in person or book an online consultation via WhatsApp video call, Dr. Muneeba and her team are available every day from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM — ready to listen, diagnose, and heal the right way.
